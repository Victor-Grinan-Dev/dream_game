- original features:
    - squads have a limited 6X6 spaces
    - each unit has a x amount of spaces it ocupies.
        infantry uses 2x2, cavalry 2x3, large unit 3x3,  vehicle 3x4, heavy vehicle 4x5, large mount 3x6,?
    - each unit will have key words based on its equipments.
        - each unit can be asigned a mount, this will give the "mount" keyword.
        - each unit with heavy armor will get the "heavy" keyword
        - each unit with ranged weapons will get the "ranged" keyword.
        - each unit on foot will get the "infantry" keyword.
        - each unit with less that halve its equipment capacity & no heavy armor equiped will get the "light" keyword
